Anna and Harper

Harper Steele and Anna Kenan founded Sassy Signs Greenville in August of 2020. Harper is the wife of David and they have 2 daughters, one at Clemson and one at USC. Harper is a care companion and has her own interior design business. Anna is the wife of Kemper and has one son at USC and a daughter in high school. She is a preschool teacher and owns a vinyl monogramming business. These good friends and neighbors saw the need to bring people together during the pandemic and sought to use their creativity and entrepreneurial spirits to do so. People weren’t able to gather in groups but still wanted birthdays and celebrations to be special and recognized. What better way to surprise your loved one than with our eye catching, unique, oversized, handmade wooden signs?

Here you can talk about the work you’ve done within your neighborhood, for friends and for family . As well as any others that you’ve done signs with. You can also thank everyone who has rented signs from you. You can also write an apppeal to new customers inviting them to use your service, this could possibly include an offer to do work with schools, club/sport teams, and possibly small businesses
